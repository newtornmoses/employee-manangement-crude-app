XPERIA

With Xperia™ Companion you can back up your Xperia™ content to your computer, or restore it from the computer to your device. You can also browse the content on your device. Update and repair Xperia™ Companion offers an easy way to install new software updates, but also to perform a software repair – a quick and easy way to solve any performance issues. Transfer content Xperia™ Companion includes a tool to easily move content like contacts, messages, calendar, multimedia and more, from an old Android or iOS device to your new Xperia™.
